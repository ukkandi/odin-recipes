Tomato Pasta

A simple pasta dish with a classic tomato-garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta (any kind)
  • 1/2 cup canned tomato sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until golden brown and fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  4. Drain pasta and add it to the sauce.
  5. Mix the pasta well with the sauce.
  6. Top with cheese if desired.
  7. Serve warm.
