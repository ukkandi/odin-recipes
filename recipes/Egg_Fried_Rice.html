Egg Fried Rice

A fast and flavorful dish using leftover rice and simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh, frozen, or canned peas (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Crack and scramble the eggs until cooked.
  3. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  4. Add peas and soy sauce, stir to combine.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking or burning.
  6. Serve hot.
