Avocado Toast

A quick and healthy breakfast with creamy avocado on toasted bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Mash the avocado in a bowl.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado on the toast.
  4. Sprinkle with salt, red pepper flakes, and lemon juice if desired.
  5. Serve immediately.
